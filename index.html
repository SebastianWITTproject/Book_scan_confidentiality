Privacy Policy

Book Scan - AI-Powered Book Analysis App

Last Updated: January 2025

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data from users.

Book Scan does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information such as names, email addresses, or device identifiers.

Local Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device:

  • Your scanned books and library
  • Favorites and personal notes
  • App preferences and settings

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

Third-Party Services

Book Scan uses the following services:

  • OpenLibrary API: To retrieve public book information
  • Google Gemini API: To provide AI-powered book analysis

These services only receive the book's ISBN and title for analysis purposes. No personal data is shared.

Data Security

Since we don't collect personal data, there's no risk of data breaches or unauthorized access to your personal information.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the App Store.
